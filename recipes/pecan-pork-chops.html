Keto Pecan-Crusted Pork Chops in the Air Fryer

Description

The air fryer makes easy work of these nutty pecan pork chops that will suit anyone following a keto lifestyle. Ground cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves pair perfectly with the flavor of the pork. Serve warm, garnished with fresh parsley. "Great juicy pork chops," says home cook Connie Horner. "Loved them!"

Ingredients

  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1 pinch ground cloves
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 ½ cups finely chopped raw pecans
  • 4 bone-in pork chops, 3/4-inch thick
  • cooking spray
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
