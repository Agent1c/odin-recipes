Air Fryer Ranch Pork Chops

Description

Pork chops are seasoned with dry ranch salad dressing mix. Feel free to experiment with different varieties of ranch mix to change the flavor completely. "Delicious!" says home cook MissRhythm. "Juicy and flavorful - a hit with the whole family!"

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, center-cut pork chops, 1-inch thick
  • cooking spray
  • 2 teaspoons dry ranch salad dressing mix (such as Hidden Valley Ranch)
